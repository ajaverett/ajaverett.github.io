Half-Baked Passion Projects

A Brief Introduction to Statistics

I wrote/compiled this book as a way to introduce people to univariate and bivariate data, distributions, and hypothesis testing. I have always been interested in statistics, and I wanted to break down these concepts in a way that was easy to understand but went into enough detail to be useful.

A Brief Introduction to Intelligence

I wrote/compiled this book as a way to introduce people to the field of intelligence research and its criticisms. I have always been interested in the field of intelligence research, and I wanted to share my knowledge with others. I also wanted to make a book that was easy but robustly cited.

Some Brief Counters around Heritability

I compiled this book as a way to counter some common claims against the idea of heritability (ratio of variance explained by genetics to total variation in a given phenotype). Understanding to two fronts to improving a phenotype (genetics and environment) is important to me.
